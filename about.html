About Ozark Aussiedoodles

Raising exceptional family companions with love, care, and dedication.

Our Story

At Ozark Aussiedoodles, we are a family-based breeder located in the heart of the Ozarks. Our passion is raising healthy, well-socialized puppies that grow into loving members of your family.

Every puppy is raised inside our home with daily interaction, enrichment, and early socialization to ensure confidence and temperament.

Our Commitment

We focus on health testing, responsible breeding practices, and matching each puppy with the right forever home. Our goal is to provide families with companions that bring joy for years to come.

We are always available to answer questions and support our puppy families long after adoption day.
